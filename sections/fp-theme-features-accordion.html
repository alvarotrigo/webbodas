Significant others

iPhone and Mac

Seamlessly integrate your iPhone with your Mac for a unified experience. Share files instantly, take calls on your Mac, and use your iPhone as a webcam. Universal Clipboard lets you copy on one device and paste on another.

iPhone and Apple Watch

Misplaced your iPhone? The latest Apple Watch models can show you its approximate distance and direction. To set up a group photo on your iPhone, join the group and use Apple Watch as a viewfinder to snap the shot. And when you take a call on your Apple Watch, just tap your iPhone to continue the conversation there.

iPhone and AirPods

Experience crystal-clear audio with seamless switching between devices. Spatial audio creates an immersive listening experience, while active noise cancellation blocks out distractions. Conversation Boost makes voices clearer in crowded environments.

iPhone and iPad

Start a project on your iPhone and finish it on your iPad with Handoff. Use your iPad as a second display with Sidecar. Draw with Apple Pencil and insert sketches directly into documents on your iPhone.
