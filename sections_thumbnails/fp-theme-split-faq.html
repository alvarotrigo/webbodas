Frequently asked questions

Trusted by over 10,000 users worldwide. We're here to answer your questions and provide the support you need.

How can I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email. If you encounter any issues, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

How do I update my profile information?

Navigate to your account settings, select "Profile", and update your personal information including name, email, and preferences. Changes are saved automatically.

How do I update my billing information?

Go to Settings > Billing and update your payment method, billing address, or view your payment history. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

How do I upload a file or document?

Click the upload button in your dashboard, select your file, and wait for the upload to complete. Supported formats include PDF, DOC, JPG, and PNG up to 10MB.

How do I delete my account?

Visit your account settings and select "Delete Account". Please note this action is permanent and will remove all your data from our system. Make sure to download any important information first.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us through email, live chat, or phone support available 24/7. Our team typically responds within 2 hours during business hours.
