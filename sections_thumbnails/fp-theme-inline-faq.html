FAQ

The FAQ section provides answers to common questions about our products, services, policies, and procedures. It's designed to help you find quick solutions and information

Q. How can I contact customer support?

A. You can reach us through email, live chat, or phone support available 24/7.

Q. How do I delete my account?

A. Visit your account settings and select the account deletion option.

Q. How can I reset my password?

A. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

Q. How do I update my profile information?

A. Navigate to account settings, select "Profile", and update your information.

Q. Which is better short term or long term?

A. The choice depends on your goals, risk tolerance, and financial situation.

Q. How do I change my email address?

A. Contact customer support via the "Help" button or email for assistance.
