Our Story

How It All Began

It was a rainy Tuesday in Dublin when two souls found each other in the most unexpected of places. Brian was reaching for the last copy of a Jane Austen novel — Megan had been eyeing it all morning.

What started as a dispute over a book became a four-hour conversation over coffee, then a walk through the park, then countless adventures across Ireland and beyond. Six years later, here we are — ready to say "I do" at the castle we dreamed about on our very first date.

Getting There

Travel & Stay

Dunhaven Castle is located in the heart of County Kerry. Here are the best ways to reach us and settle in for the weekend.

By Air

Kerry Airport (KIR) is 45 minutes away. Cork Airport (ORK) is a scenic 2-hour drive. Dublin Airport (DUB) is 4 hours with stunning views en route.

By Car

We recommend renting a car to explore Kerry at your own pace. Free parking is available at the castle. We advise arriving the day before.

Shuttle Service

We are arranging complimentary shuttles from Killarney Town Centre at 3:00 PM and returning at midnight. Please indicate in your RSVP.

Got Questions?

FAQs

Is Dunhaven Castle child-friendly?

Children are warmly welcome at the ceremony and cocktail hour. The formal dinner is adults only (18+). We recommend arranging childcare for the evening. A babysitting service is available at the hotel upon request.

What is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by June 1, 2026. This helps us finalise numbers with the venue and caterers. If your plans change after this date, please get in touch as soon as possible.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We are having an unplugged ceremony — please keep phones away during the vows so everyone can be present. Our photographer will capture every moment beautifully. After the ceremony, snap away!

Will there be parking at the castle?

Yes, free parking is available at the castle grounds. However, we encourage guests to use the shuttle service or share rides if you plan to enjoy the bar.

What should I do if I have a food allergy?

Please note any dietary requirements or allergies in your RSVP form. The castle kitchen is fully equipped to accommodate most dietary needs including gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free options.
