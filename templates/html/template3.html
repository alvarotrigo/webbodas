How It All Began

Our Love Story

It started with a chance encounter at a farmers market on a dewy Saturday morning. Liam was reaching for the last bunch of sunflowers — Olivia's favorite — and their hands met among the petals.

Three years, two rescue dogs, and one unforgettable hike to a waterfall later, Liam dropped to one knee in the golden hour light and asked the only question that mattered.

Now, surrounded by the rustling trees and open skies we love so much, we invite you to celebrate the next chapter of our story.
