Your Questions Answered

This section aims to provide clear and comforting answers to common inquiries. Our goal is to support you in your journey and reassure you about the process of seeking help.

Q.Q. How can I get in touch with Dr. Elena?

A.A. You can connect with Dr. Elena through our online booking system, or feel free to reach out via email to schedule a consultation.

Q.Q. What can I expect during my first session?

A.A. Your first session will focus on understanding your unique needs and establishing a safe space for open dialogue about your experiences.

Q.Q. How long are therapy sessions?

A.A. Each session typically lasts 50 minutes, allowing ample time for exploration and reflection.

Q.Q. How do I know if therapy is right for me?

A.A. If you're feeling overwhelmed by anxiety, stress, or facing significant life changes, therapy can provide valuable support and guidance.

Q.Q. What should I do if I need to cancel an appointment?

A.A. Please give us a call or send an email at least 24 hours in advance if you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment.

Q.Q. Is my information confidential?

A.A. Yes, your privacy is paramount. All information shared in sessions remains confidential, creating a safe environment for your healing.
