How it all began

Three years, countless late-night conversations, two rescued cats, and one unforgettable rooftop proposal later — here they are, ready to begin the next chapter surrounded by the people they love most.

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know

What is the dress code?

Black tie optional. Think elegant evening wear — long gowns, cocktail dresses, dark suits, or tuxedos. We want you to feel as glamorous as the night itself.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Please check your invitation. If it says "and guest," you're welcome to bring someone special. If you're unsure, don't hesitate to reach out to us directly.

Is there parking available?

Valet parking is available at the venue entrance. We also recommend taxis, rideshare, or the subway — the venue is steps from several stations.

Will there be dietary options?

Absolutely. Please mention any dietary requirements in your RSVP and we'll ensure there are beautiful options for every guest.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We kindly ask for an unplugged ceremony — our photographer will capture every moment. Feel free to snap away during the reception and party!
