Helpful Information

Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

Doors open at 3:00 PM for a welcome aperitivo. The ceremony begins promptly at 4:00 PM, so we ask all guests to be seated by 3:50 PM. There is ample parking on-site or you can use the shuttle from Hotel Palazzo Siena.

Are children welcome at the wedding?

Children are warmly welcome at our wedding. A dedicated kids' table with activities and early dinner will be arranged. Please indicate the number of children and ages in your RSVP so we can prepare accordingly.

What is the dress code exactly?

Garden Formal means elegant and polished but with a relaxed, natural feel. For ladies: midi or maxi dresses, elegant separates in earthy tones, sage, blush, sand or stone. For gentlemen: linen suits or smart trousers with a shirt. Please avoid bright white, ivory, or jet black. The venue is outdoor and on grass, so we'd suggest avoiding stiletto heels.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available at Villa Bellamore. If you plan to enjoy our open bar, we strongly encourage you to use our complimentary shuttle service running to and from Hotel Palazzo Siena in Siena city centre.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We kindly ask for an unplugged ceremony — please keep phones away during the vows so you can be fully present with us. Our professional photographer will capture every magical moment and photos will be shared with all guests after the wedding. Feel free to snap away at the reception!

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Our caterers are experienced in accommodating all dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and all major allergies. Please note your requirements in the RSVP form and we'll ensure you're beautifully catered for.
