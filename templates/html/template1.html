The Story of Us

From strangers to soulmates
☕
Spring 2020 — The Meeting

A Rainy Day in Florence

Both reaching for the last almond croissant at Caffè Rivoire. Mateo let Isabella have it. She offered to share. They talked until the rain stopped — and then kept talking.

✨
Summer 2020 — First Date

Dinner Under the Stars

A rooftop trattoria, homemade pasta, and a view of the Duomo. Mateo accidentally knocked over a candle. The waiter saved them. They laughed about it for weeks.

🏡
Autumn 2022 — Moving In

Our Little Corner of the World

A cozy apartment with too many plants, a cat named Espresso, and the understanding that Mateo always gets to choose the movie on Fridays.

💍
December 2025 — The Proposal

Back Where It Began

Mateo took Isabella back to that same café in Florence. Same table, same croissant — but this time with a ring hidden inside the napkin. She said yes before he even finished asking.

Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know
Can I bring a plus-one?

Please check your invitation for details. If your invite says "and guest," you're welcome to bring someone! If you're unsure, reach out to us — we'll figure it out together.

Is there transportation to the venue?

Yes! We'll have shuttle buses running between the hotels and the villa. Schedules will be shared closer to the date. If you prefer to drive, there's ample parking on site.

Are children welcome?

While we love your little ones, we've opted for an adults-only celebration to allow everyone to fully enjoy the evening. We hope you understand!

What if it rains?

The villa has beautiful indoor spaces as backup. Rain or shine, it's going to be magical — we've planned for both scenarios!
