Your Questions Answered with Care

This section addresses common inquiries to provide clarity and reassurance about the therapeutic process and what to expect during your journey.

Q.Q. How can I reach Dr. Elena Rivera?

A.A. You can easily get in touch with Dr. Rivera by filling out the contact form on this website, or by calling her office directly.

Q.Q. What should I expect during my first session?

A.A. The first session is an opportunity for you to share your concerns and for Dr. Rivera to understand your needs and goals in a supportive environment.

Q.Q. How long are therapy sessions?

A.A. Each session typically lasts 50 minutes, allowing ample time for discussion and reflection.

Q.Q. How often should I attend sessions?

A.A. Frequency can vary based on individual needs, but many clients find weekly sessions beneficial in the beginning.

Q.Q. Can therapy help with life transitions?

A.A. Absolutely. Therapy offers a safe space to navigate changes and challenges, providing support as you adjust to new circumstances.

Q.Q. How do I schedule my first appointment?

A.A. You can schedule your initial consultation by using the booking form on our website or contacting the office directly.
