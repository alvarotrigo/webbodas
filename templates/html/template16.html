Questions

Frequently Asked

Is the ceremony outdoors?

Yes! Both the ceremony and reception are outdoors at the Amoudi Bay Estate. We have an indoor contingency for unexpected weather.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to the intimate venue, we've carefully curated our guest list. Your invitation will state clearly whether you have a plus one.

Are children welcome?

Children are welcome! We'll have a dedicated kids' area during the reception. Please indicate on your RSVP how many children are attending.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We're having an unplugged ceremony — phones away during the vows please! Our photographers will capture every moment and share them with you after.
