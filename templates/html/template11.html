Getting Here

Accommodation & Transport

We want to make sure your journey to and from our celebration is as smooth as possible.

Recommended Hotels

We have reserved a room block at The Grand Ashford Hotel (5 min from venue) and Countryside Inn (10 min). Use code SOPHIEWILL26 for a 15% discount when booking directly.

Shuttle Service

We are providing a complimentary shuttle from Ashford International Station to the venue at 3:00 PM. Return shuttles at 12:30 AM. Indicate your need in the RSVP form.

Parking

Free parking is available on-site for up to 200 vehicles. The car park will be supervised throughout the event and is a short walk from the chapel entrance.

Local Taxis

Ashford Cars (01233 123456) and Kent Cabs (01233 654321) know the venue well and offer competitive rates. Pre-booking is recommended for late evening returns.

Dress Code

Garden Chic

We'd love for guests to embrace the romantic garden setting. Think florals, pastels, light fabrics. Please avoid white or ivory.

For the Ladies

Garden-length or midi dresses in soft florals and pastels. Wedges or block heels recommended as parts of the venue are on grass.

For the Gentlemen

Smart suits in light tones — dusty blue, linen, stone or sage — with a crisp shirt and optional tie in complementary pastel shades.
