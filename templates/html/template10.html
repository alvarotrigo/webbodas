Our Story

Born from a
chance encounter
in Paris

It was a rainy Tuesday evening in the Marais when Laurent nearly knocked Isabelle's coffee from her hands outside Café de Flore. He apologized in three languages before she laughed and invited him to sit.

Three years, twelve countries, and one very patient dog later, he proposed on the cliffs of Étretat as the sun set over Normandy.

Now we invite you to join us as we begin the most beautiful chapter yet.

Questions

Everything
you may wonder

Is the ceremony outdoors?+

Yes, the ceremony will take place in the rose garden. In case of rain, we have a covered terrace as a backup. We recommend comfortable shoes suitable for gravel paths.

When should I RSVP by?+

Please confirm your attendance by July 1st, 2026. This allows us to finalize catering and seating arrangements with our venue.

Is there a shuttle from the nearest city?+

Yes! We have arranged a complimentary shuttle from Aix-en-Provence city centre departing at 3:15 PM and returning at 1:30 AM. Indicate your need in the RSVP form.

Can I bring a plus one?+

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate the guests listed on your invitation. If your invitation includes a plus one, please fill in their details separately in the RSVP form.

Are children welcome?+

We love your little ones! Children are welcome at the ceremony. For the reception, we ask that children under 5 be supervised by a parent or carer at all times.

Will photos be shared after the wedding?+

Absolutely. We will update this page with a gallery of professional photos within 8 weeks of the wedding. You will receive a link by email to relive every magical moment.
