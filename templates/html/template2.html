Our Journey

Our Love Story

From the first hello to forever

Spring 2021 — The Meeting

A Sunday at the Botanical Garden

Both sketching the same magnolia tree from opposite benches. Olivia noticed James first — his pencil moved like a conductor's baton. He noticed her laugh when a robin stole her eraser. Coffee followed. Then dinner. Then every day after.

Summer 2022 — The Adventure

Lost in the Lavender Fields

A road trip through the countryside that was supposed to be three days turned into two weeks. They got lost in lavender fields in Provence, danced in the rain in a tiny village, and knew — this was it.

December 2025 — The Proposal

Under the Wisteria

James brought Olivia back to that same botanical garden, to the same bench where it all began. This time the magnolia was bare, but a ring sparkled in the winter light. She said yes before her tears could say it first.

Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know

Can I bring a plus-one?

Please check your invitation for details. If it says "and guest," absolutely! If you're unsure, just reach out to us.

What should I wear?

Garden formal. Think flowing dresses, linen suits, and comfortable shoes for the lawn. Soft pastels and earth tones welcome — please avoid white or ivory.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, complimentary parking is available on the estate grounds. We'll also have a shuttle running between the recommended hotels and the venue.

Are children welcome?

We've planned an adults-only evening so everyone can relax and enjoy. We hope you understand and treat it as a night off!

What if it rains?

The estate has a gorgeous glass conservatory as a backup. Rain or shine, it will be beautiful — we've planned for every scenario!
