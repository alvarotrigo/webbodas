Frequently Asked Questions

We've answered the questions guests ask most. Don't see yours? Drop us a message below.

What is the dress code?
Smart elegant attire is required. Please avoid white, ivory, or cream clothing out of respect for the bride. We'd love to see florals and soft colours for the outdoor ceremony.
Are children welcome?
We love children and they are absolutely welcome! A dedicated kids' corner with entertainment will be set up during the reception. Please note this when completing your RSVP.
Is parking available?
As the venue is in central Venice, we recommend arriving by water taxi or vaporetto. We've arranged shuttle boats from Piazzale Roma for guests. Details in your invitation.
Can I take photos during the ceremony?
We're having an unplugged ceremony — please put phones away and enjoy the moment with us. Our photographer will capture everything beautifully. Photos are welcome during the reception!
What time should I arrive?
Please arrive by 3:00 pm for welcome drinks on the garden terrace. The ceremony begins promptly at 4:00 pm, so please plan to be seated by 3:45 pm.
Will there be accommodation nearby?
Yes! Hotel Adami has reserved a block of rooms at a special rate for our guests. Please mention "Jennifer & Oliver Wedding" when booking. We recommend reserving early as availability is limited.
