Our Journey

A Love Story
Written in Passport Stamps

From strangers in a hostel to partners for life

What started as a chance meeting at a backpackers' hostel in Lisbon turned into the greatest adventure of our lives. Here are the places that wrote our love story.

2019
Lisbon, Portugal

The First Hello

A crowded hostel rooftop, a shared bottle of wine, and a sunset over the Tagus River. We didn't know it yet, but this was the beginning of everything.

2020
Tokyo, Japan

Lost & Found

Navigating the neon-lit streets of Shinjuku, we got completely lost — and realized we never wanted to find our way without each other.

2022
Santorini, Greece

The Question

Under the most spectacular sunset either of us had ever seen, with the caldera shimmering below, James asked the question that changed everything.

"We travel not to escape life, but for life not to escape us."
— Our motto since day one
