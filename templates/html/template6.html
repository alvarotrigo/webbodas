Our Journey

Our Story

2019

The Meet-Cute

We both reached for the last oat milk latte at a Brooklyn coffee shop. Jake offered to share. Mia said yes. The rest is history.

2021

Moving In

After two years of stealing each other's hoodies, we made it official and got our own tiny apartment (with a great view of a brick wall).

2023

The Adventure

We quit our jobs for three months and backpacked through Southeast Asia. Somewhere between Bali and Bangkok, we knew this was forever.

2025

The Proposal

Jake proposed on the rooftop where we had our first date — with a ring, a speech he'd memorized (mostly), and happy tears all around.
