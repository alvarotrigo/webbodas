The Timeline of Us

How We Got Here
(Spoiler: It's Adorable)

A love story told in awkward moments
🎉
2020 — The Meet-Cute

Wrong Party, Right Person

Tom showed up to the wrong birthday party. Mia was at the right one. They met in the hallway arguing over whose Uber was whose. Sparks flew. So did some confetti.

🍕
2020 — First Date

Pizza & Honesty

Their first date was at a pizza place. Tom ate 7 slices. Mia was impressed. They talked for 4 hours about everything from conspiracy theories to their grandmothers' cookie recipes.

✈️
2022 — The Test

Surviving IKEA Together

They assembled a KALLAX shelf with zero arguments. At that point, they knew this was true love. Moving in together was the easy part after that.

💍
2025 — The Proposal

The Burrito Incident

On a random Tuesday, Tom hid the ring inside Mia's favorite burrito. She bit into foil. Panicked. Then cried happy tears for 20 minutes straight. She said yes (obviously).
