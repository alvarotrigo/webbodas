Who We Are

Meet the Couple

A Love Rooted in Nature

Sophie is a botanical illustrator with a passion for wildflowers and old greenhouses. James is an architect who finds beauty in the way light moves through glass structures. They bonded over a shared love of garden walks, afternoon tea, and the quiet magic of watching things grow.

Together, they've built a life full of small wonders — weekend markets, rainy-day baking, and an ever-growing collection of houseplants. Now they're ready to celebrate their love with the people who matter most.

Our Story

How We Met

Autumn 2021

The Greenhouse Meeting

We both ducked into the same greenhouse during a sudden London rainstorm. James was sketching the ironwork; Sophie was drawing a rare orchid. A shared bench, a shared laugh about the weather, and a coffee at the garden café that lasted three hours.

Spring 2023

The Cotswolds Trip

Our first proper holiday together — a cottage in the Cotswolds with a wildflower garden. We spent the days exploring hidden paths and the evenings cooking together. By the end of that week, we both knew this was something extraordinary.

Winter 2025

The Proposal

During the winter lantern festival at Kew, among thousands of twinkling lights and the scent of pine, James got down on one knee beside the lake. Sophie cried happy tears and said yes before the question was fully out.
